Pancakes

Description

Fluffy and delicious homemade pancakes perfect for breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Maple syrup (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in milk, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Heat a pan or griddle over medium heat.
  4. Pour batter and cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  5. Serve hot with butter and syrup.

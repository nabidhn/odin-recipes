Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Cook the meat and make the sauce
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese
  4. Bake in the oven

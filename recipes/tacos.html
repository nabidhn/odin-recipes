Tacos

Description

These simple tacos are made with seasoned meat and fresh toppings for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Soft or hard taco shells
  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Taco seasoning
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Brown the meat in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and cook according to packet instructions.
  3. Warm taco shells.
  4. Fill with meat and your favorite toppings.
  5. Serve immediately.
